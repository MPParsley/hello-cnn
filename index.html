Hello CNN

A convolutional neural network that learns to read handwritten digits — running entirely in your browser.

What is a CNN?

🔍

Convolve

Small filters slide across the image detecting edges, curves, and textures.

⬇

Pool

Downsampling keeps the strongest signals and discards noise.

🧠

Learn

Repeated layers build up from edges to shapes to digit identities.

🎯

Classify

A final dense layer maps the learned features to digit probabilities.
